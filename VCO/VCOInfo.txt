Simple VCO will outup a square and saw wave. It has cv input for pitch and knobs to controll the following;
Tune,Fine Tune, and Pulse Width
There is also an interal trimmer that should be tuned before final asembly 

simple VCO - saw/sqr

3 x 100k potentiometers
