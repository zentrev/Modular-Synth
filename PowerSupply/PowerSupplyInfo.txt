Power supply taking DC in and turning it into a flying bus with +12, -12, +5, and GND

Power Supply:
DC wall Adapter
DC Jack
6 x 4700 uf electrolyic capacitors 35+v
2 x 220 uf electrolyic capacitors
1 x +12 voltage regulator
1 x -12 voltage regulator
1 x +125c voltage regulator
3 x rectifier diode (1N4007)
capacitors
heatsinks
thermalpaste
switch
output header
floating bus (https://modularsynthlab.com/product/eurorack-flying-bus-board-cable-8x-or-10x-16pin-connectors/)
